What it does

One click. A whole tournament.

You hit "Run the swarm," and the app builds rosters, simulates the bracket, narrates it, stores it, and emails the verdict.

1

Build rosters

Rated players per nation, with AI-generated personas & taglines.

2

Simulate

2,000 Monte Carlo runs per round + one featured champion playthrough.

3

Narrate

Claude writes commentary, player flavor, and the model's verdict.

4

Persist & recap

Results saved to Supabase; a recap email goes out via Resend.

The design principle

The math is deterministic.
The AI is just the voice.

⚙ Deterministic code owns

State & scores

  • Goals, winners, penalties — from a seeded engine
  • Monte Carlo odds, reproducible every run
  • Everything written to the database
✦ Claude owns

The narrative

  • Roster flavor & player personas
  • Live commentary on each match
  • The verdict + recap email copy

The agent never alters a final score after the simulator produces it.

Under the hood

Why the odds are trustworthy.

No vibes — a real expected-goals model, sampled thousands of times.

2,000
sims / round
Conditional win % per opponent, multiplied into a cumulative title chance.
xG
Poisson model
Expected goals from each side's attack ÷ defense, then sampled to a scoreline.
1 seed
fully reproducible
mulberry32 RNG — same seed, same tournament, every single time.
const xg = BASE_XG * Math.pow(attack / defense, SENSITIVITY);   // then poisson(xg, rng)
